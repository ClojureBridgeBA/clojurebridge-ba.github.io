Clojure Programming Workshops

English: ClojureBridge is a free Clojure programming workshop for women and marginalized groups. Especially for those with just a little or no programming experience. Next workshop: January 1-1 at Wunderlist.

What is ClojureBridge?

English: ClojureBridge aims to increase diversity within the Clojure community by offering free, beginner-friendly Clojure programming workshops for women and marginalized groups.
