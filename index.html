Clojure Programming Workshops

English: ClojureBridge is a free Clojure programming workshop for women. Especially for those with just a little or no programming experience.
Next workshop: January 22 and 23, 2016 in Berlin.

Deutsch: ClojureBridge ist ein kostenloser Programmier-Workshop für Frauen. Besonders für diejenigen mit keiner oder wenig Erfahrung im Programmieren.
Nächster Workshop: 22. und 23. Januar in Berlin.

Apply now: / Bewirb dich jetzt:

Workshop

English: ClojureBridge aims to increase diversity within the Clojure community by offering free, beginner-friendly Clojure programming workshops for women.

Clojure is a modern programming language. We will use it to introduce you to fundamental programming concepts and build a creative app.

Workshop facts:

  • January 22 and 23, 2016 in Berlin

  • Friday: evening from 6 PM

  • Saturday: 9 AM to 5 PM

  • Vegan food and drinks will be provided

  • The event is free of charge

Each participant needs to bring a laptop with a fairly recent operating system (Mac, Linux or Windows). If you have questions about your hardware or you don’t have a laptop, don’t hesitate to send an email to the organizers before the event!

Deutsch: ClojureBridge möchte die Vielfalt in der Clojure Community fördern und bietet deswegen kostenlose, anfängerinnenfreundliche Programmier-Workshops für Frauen an.
